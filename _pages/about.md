Greetings! I'm a Robotics Engineer at Dyson's Robot Learning Team, led by Stephen James. My journey began at the Technical University of Berlin, where I recently graduated, with my master thesis on In-Hand Manipulation through Wrist Motions under the guidance of Prof. Oliver Brock and Adrian Sieler.

My professional voyage has been a thrilling ride through robotics, spanning humanoids, autonomous driving, and soft robots. Beyond the lab, I find joy in yoga, cross-fit, chess battles on chess.com, and sports like table tennis, badminton, and basketball. I'm also a budding guitarist and a food enthusiast, always exploring the latest in tech and indulging in the captivating world of anime. Let's connect and explore the exciting realm of robotics together! 🤖🚀🎸🍳🎮
